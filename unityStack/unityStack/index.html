FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
+
Simple websites typically take 5–10 days. Standard multi-page websites take 10–21 days. Complex systems like school management platforms or e-commerce stores may take 30–60 days depending on scope. We'll give you a clear timeline before starting your project.
Do you offer payment in installments?
+
Yes! For most projects, we accept 50% upfront to start the work and the remaining 50% upon completion before handover. For larger projects, a 3-installment plan can be arranged. We're flexible and always happy to discuss what works best for you.
Will I be able to update the website myself?
+
Absolutely. Depending on the type of site, we either build a user-friendly admin panel that lets you update content yourself, or we provide simple training and documentation. We can also offer a maintenance package if you'd prefer us to handle all updates.
Do you help with domain and hosting?
+
Yes — we handle everything from domain registration to hosting setup, SSL certificate installation, and full deployment. We'll help you choose the right hosting plan based on your site's needs and budget.
What information do I need to provide to start?
+
To get started, we'll need a brief description of your business or organization, your goals for the website, any design preferences or inspiration sites you like, your logo and brand colors (if available), and the content (text and images) you want on the site. We'll guide you through the rest in our initial consultation.
Do you build websites for NGOs and religious organizations?
+
Yes, absolutely. We have experience working with NGOs, faith-based organizations, mosques, churches, and community groups. We understand your unique needs and offer special pricing for non-profit organizations.
